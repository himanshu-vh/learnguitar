About This Tool

This interactive guitar fretboard visualizer helps you explore different scales and modes across the entire fretboard. Whether you're a beginner learning your first scale or an advanced player exploring exotic modes, this tool makes it easy to visualize note patterns and improve your understanding of music theory.

How to Use

  1. Select a root note from the dropdown (e.g., C, D, E, etc.)
  2. Choose a scale type (Major, Minor, Pentatonic, etc.)
  3. Click "Visualize" to see the scale on the fretboard
  4. Green circles show all the notes in your selected scale
